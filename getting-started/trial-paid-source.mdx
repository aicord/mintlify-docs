# Trial vs Paid vs Source

This section prevents support confusion during pre-sales.

## Scope policy

- Main documentation flow is **Paid**.
- Trial is only covered as a feature comparison.
- Source package details are high-level and contract-dependent.

## Comparison

| Area | Trial | Paid | Source |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Core chat | Limited | Full | Full |
| Panel | Usually limited/disabled | Full | Full |
| Automation depth | Limited | Full | Full + custom |
| Support priority | Low/Community | Standard | Highest/Custom |
| Commercial use | Restricted by terms | Allowed by terms | Allowed by terms |

## Recommendation

For production and client delivery, use Paid with AiCordCloud integration.
